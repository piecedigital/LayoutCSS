#Widths and Heights

##Available styles

* **.width-x_x**
* **.min-width-x_x**
* **.max-width-x_x**
* **.height-x_x**
* **.min-height-x_x**
* **.max-height-x_x**

> Sets the width/height, minimum width/height, or maximum width/height of a given element equal to x.x * 16px

> Goes up to 80.0

######Usage
``` html
<div class="min-width-1_0">...</div>
```
